        <h2 class="section-header">Innovation Timeline</h2>
        
        <h3 class="subsection-header">Hashtag Campaign Innovation: #AskSeaWorld (2015)</h3>
        <p>Pioneered a landmark example of strategic hashtag response and real-time content creation that demonstrated the power of coordinated social media activism:</p>
        <ul class="feature-list">
            <li>Developed rapid response visual content creation system for maximum impact</li>
            <li>Coordinated multi-channel distribution strategy across platforms</li>
            <li>Leveraged existing email lists for amplified reach and engagement</li>
            <li>Created shareable, emotion-driven content optimized for social platforms</li>
        </ul>

        <h3 class="subsection-header">Video Content Innovation: Britches Animal Testing Story (2016)</h3>
        <p>Led the evolution into video content strategy, achieving industry recognition and record-breaking engagement rates:</p>
        <ul class="feature-list">
            <li>Pioneered rapid video content creation ahead of market adoption</li>
            <li>Developed efficient workflows for real-time video editing and deployment</li>
            <li>Implemented multi-platform video optimization techniques</li>
            <li>Created scalable video production system for consistent quality</li>
        </ul>

        <p>The 2015-2016 period marked significant evolution in social media strategy:</p>
        <ul class="feature-list">
            <li>Shift from reactive posting to coordinated campaign strategies</li>
            <li>Early adoption of video content before widespread platform support</li>
            <li>Development of emotion-driven content frameworks</li>
            <li>Integration of multi-channel distribution approaches</li>
        </ul>

        <h3 class="subsection-header">Strategic Evolution</h3>
        <ul class="feature-list">

        </ul>
