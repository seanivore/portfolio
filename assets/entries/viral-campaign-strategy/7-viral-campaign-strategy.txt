### Innovative Social Strategy
Created a viral Facebook Frame campaign that generated significant engagement and press coverage:
- Developed a strategic Facebook Frame that went viral among target audience
- Generated significant press coverage, including feature in Outdoor Life magazine
- Successfully hijacked the #shootselfiesnotanimals hashtag on Instagram
- Created widespread engagement and discussion across social platforms

### Campaign Highlights
- Award-winning humor-based viral video reaching 100M+ views
- Integrated campaign finalist in Shorty Social Good Awards
- Innovative use of new social media tools to create trending topics
- Strategic content planning and distribution across multiple platforms
- Successful engagement of diverse audience demographics
- Generated significant earned media coverage
