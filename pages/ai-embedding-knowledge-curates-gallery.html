Technical Analysis

Feedback Loop

This technique involves repeatedly taking a video signal and sending it back into the system, often resulting in distorted and layered images. An example can be visually similar to echoing effects in sound, which can create three-dimensional visual experiences.

Pixel Sorting

This method rearranges pixels based on certain criteria, creating a visual distortion that can resemble abstract waveforms. Works showcasing pixel sorting often evoke feelings of disarray while tugging at the viewer's cognition of structure within visual art.

Data Moshing

Data moshing is a method that disrupts codec processes to produce unexpected visual glitches. Examples can often depict sudden transitions between scenes or fragments of images being reassembled in distorted ways, evoking the chaotic beauty of imperfection.
