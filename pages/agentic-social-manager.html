AI-Powered Social Media Management System

A sophisticated implementation of AI-driven social media management using Make.com workflows, demonstrating how automation and AI can create engaging, consistent social media presence while maintaining authentic interactions and strategic content deployment.

Automated Image Management

Automated scenario that found relevant images for what was needed to post from our ArtMovementMuseum Print Shop. The system handles image selection, cropping, sizing, and caption generation using vision AI and context-aware content creation.

Automated production of image posts with generatively written text to promote articles. The system integrates vision AI review, content generation, and design services for cohesive social media content.

Automated creation and management of tracked links for analytics across all social media platforms, enabling data-driven optimization of content strategy.

Automated lookbook promotion system that generates fresh angles and content variations for consistent, engaging social media presence across platforms.

Sophisticated engagement workflow that combines vision AI analysis with AI-generated responses, creating authentic and informed interactions with influencer content.

Key Takeaways

This project demonstrates the power of combining AI and automation in social media management. By leveraging vision AI, natural language processing, and automated workflows, we've created a system that maintains authentic engagement while operating at scale. The success in generating informed, context-aware content and managing multi-platform presence showcases how AI can enhance rather than replace human creativity in social media management.
